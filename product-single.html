Blumea leaves (Sambong) display a variety of biological activities such as antitumor, hepatoprotective, antioxidant, antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, anti-melanogenic, platelet aggregation, wound healing, and anti-obesity properties. It is used as herbal treatment for kidney stones, cuts or wounds, rheumatism, diarrhoea, spasms, colds, coughs, and hypertension. The Philippine Council for Health Research and Development has developed the technology for sambong herbal medicine tablets particularly for anti-urolithiatic purposes which have been available in the market for such indications. Consumption of sambong tea or leaf infusion can also help dissolve kidney stones and prevent urolith formation. Blumea balsamifera is a flowering plant that grows abundantly in Southeast Asia and India, in open fields, grasslands, and distributed areas at low and medium altitudes.
